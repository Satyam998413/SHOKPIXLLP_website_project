about us

Sun and Sand Facility Management Services is a part of the Dubai based SASD Group of Companies. SASFM provides a wide array of services including but not limited to cleaning, MEP, civil & all kinds of maintenance (Hard & Soft) services. SASFM is a results-driven company committed to leading the regional facilities management (FM) industry. We plan to achieve this feat by delivering an experience to our clients that goes above & beyond expectations. We are driven by technology and sustainability to offer the right solutions to clients. We are known for our professionalism and our reliable services across the UAE and for our excellence in all aspects of facilities management.

We offer a complete range of services at a very cost-effective price to meet our valuable customers’ specific requirements. We have a comprehensive approach to facility management with broad experience and expertise in offices, residential, institutes & retail properties. We strive for sustainable improvement over the long-term. Our team proactively identifies areas to reduce cost and implements new processes and upgrades in ways that balance upfront costs with efficiency gains without sacrificing the safety or experience of your employees.Today the name & business strength of “Sun and Sand Facility Management Services” is due to the professional nature owned by its management and highly equipped staff.
